Welcome to the Family Tree App

Create and manage your family tree with ease. Add family members, view relationships, and explore your family history.

How to Use the Family Tree

1. View Your Family Tree

Click on "Family Tree" in the navigation menu to see your family tree organized by generations. The tree automatically arranges family members based on their relationships.

Go to Family Tree

2. Add a Family Member

On the Family Tree page, click the green "+" button in the bottom right corner to add a new family member.

  • Required: First Name and Last Name
  • Optional: Middle Initial, Birth Date, Parents, Spouse
  • Tip: Enter parent names as "First Last" (e.g., "John Smith")
  • Tip: Enter spouse name as "First Last" (e.g., "Jane Doe")

3. View a Person's Profile

Click on any person's card in the family tree to view their detailed profile page, including:

  • Birth date
  • Parents and children
  • Spouse information
  • Historical fun fact based on their birthday

4. Edit or Remove a Person

On a person's profile page, you can:

  • Click "Edit This Person" to modify their information
  • Click "Remove This Person" to delete them from the tree

5. Search for Family Members (WIP)

Use the "Search" page to quickly find family members by name.
